Getting started managing your token sale

The token dashboard is where you can easily view and manage your token sales.

Here are some tips to get you started:

  1. Click the
    icon to view and share your contract (token sale) address so investors of your token sale can participate and buy-in to your sale.
  2. Click the
    icon to view and share your wallet address to be able to receive tokens and Ether outside of your sale.
